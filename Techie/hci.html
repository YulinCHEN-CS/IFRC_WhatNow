hci

Sketch

After gathering the user requirements and using personas/scenarios to better understand our users, we drew up several sketches to explore ideas on how our web application would look like.

Sketch expressed the Feed and Dashboard (including contents, Audit log and Bulk upload) UI

Prototype

We then came up with a prototype using Figma to further explore the feasibility and usability of our sketch. The pictures shown are the web pages with the key functionalities and requirements. The arrows represents a link to a new web page after the user clicks on that particular text or icon.

Prototype-evaluation

We chose to carry out heuristics evaluation as it is quick to complete in our limited time.

Heuristic Problems solutions Severity
Highlight The option buttons on left hand side of dashboard are not highlighted enough, which may be hard for users to find them. Move them to the middle and change the color 1
Aesthetic The upper labels on Home Page are a little bit messy. Change another fonts and change colors 3
Simplicity The label of left hand side buttons on dashboard conflicts with content button, which may cause confusion for user. Remove unnecessary icon 2

Prototype-improvement

Our project underwent an iteration in the development life cycle as we revised the design for our prototype according to the evaluation results.
